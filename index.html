Hi, I am currently a Master's student in Cartography and Geographical Information System at LIESMARS, Wuhan University, supervised by Prof. Zhen Dong, Associate Researcher Fuxun Liang and Prof. Bisheng Yang.
My research interests encompass 3D city models, building facade optimization, urban morphology, urban microclimate, urban building energy modeling, and 3D GIS.
Currently, I focus on combining the Vertical Greenery Systems with 3D city models to mitigate urban heat island, improve urban thermal comfort, and reduce energy consumption.

Research Interests

  • Building Facade Optimization
  • Urban Morphology
  • Urban Microclimate
  • Urban Building Energy Modeling
  • Vertical Greenery Systems(couple with 3D city models)
  • 3D GIS
